About the Event

The Tech & Innovation Summit 2025 brings together global and regional experts to explore emerging technologies such as artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, IoT, robotics, cloud engineering, and digital product design.

This three-day summit includes keynote sessions, hands-on workshops, product demos, networking lounges, and startup showcases.

  • Cybersecurity Live Demonstrations

  • Women in Tech Leadership Panels
